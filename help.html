Stunt Planning And Recording – Help & About

About SPAR

SPAR was created out of the need for a simple way to document choreography without breaking flow. As performers, we found it frustrating to swap a sword for a clipboard or laptop every few moves just to take notes. We wanted something that could be used one-handed, even on a phone, while holding a weapon in the other hand.

This tool is the result — fast, repeatable, consistent, and designed specifically for the unique demands of stunt performance and fight choreography.

Purpose

SPAR is designed to choreograph, document, and archive fight sequences, covering footwork, weapon choreography, direction of energy, and stage direction. It provides a clean, structured way to record complex custom choreography while remaining easy enough to use during live rehearsals.

Usage Instructions

When to use

SPAR is best suited for documenting staged fight choreography between 2–5 combatants.

Setup

  1. Open the setup page.
  2. Enter the fight name (editable later in the spreadsheet).
  3. Enter the author(s).
  4. Select the number of combatants (not editable once set).
  5. For each fighter, configure:
    • Hands – number of weapon columns required (1–4).
    • Weapon class – determines dropdown options (custom text is always allowed).
  6. Click Go to build the fight spreadsheet.

Recording

The fight spreadsheet is divided into structured columns. Here’s what each area represents:

  • Footwork – movement, orientation, and steps.
  • Hand # – weapon or attack actions.
  • {e} – “direction of energy,” meaning how the combatant is leaning or what story/emotion motivates the move.
  • Notes – extra comments, context, or instructions.

Example layout of the SPAR spreadsheet.

Use Add Row to continue adding choreography steps. Use Add Break to insert a shaded divider row (for dialogue, beat breaks, or blocks of choreography).

Exporting

Click Export to save your fight as an Excel (.xlsx) file. The default filename comes from your fight title, but you can rename it before saving.

Important Note

SPAR does not save your work automatically. Closing or refreshing the page will clear your choreography. Save early, save often!

Free Use

SPAR was built for our own stunt team, but it is free for anyone to use. If SPAR helps your team, let us know! Feedback is always appreciated, and kind words go a long way.

Building, maintaining, and hosting SPAR takes time and resources, so if your team benefits from it, we welcome donations — but encouragement and stories of how you’ve used it are just as valuable.

If your team is interested in a similar tool customized for your workflow, we are open to collaborating on tailored versions of SPAR.
